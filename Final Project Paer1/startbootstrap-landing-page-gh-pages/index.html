About Us

Benelux mission is to spread the importance of helping each other within our community and world wide. With Benelux we can find the exact resources that are needed and health issues that are occuring in different countries. To provide a much quicker and effective way of reaching out and providing the help and hope that is waiting for our come.

How to Help

With Benelux you can navigate through the map and click on the country or area that you want to help out in. Once you click on the country, the resources that are needed in that specific country will appear. Along with the top diseases in that country. The purpose of this is to inform what exactly is needed and what we can do to make sure that the community is receiving the needed medical care for their illnesses. The names of other helping organizations will appear; that way you can see what their role is. It's important to bring awareness to these problems and find a solution, making it accessible for everyone to have the medications and resources that are out there.
